[1] For the most part, President Obama declared that his main focuses are correcting our economy and providing Americans, especially middle class, with more jobs [2] He laid out a more liberal vision for the United States that incorporated better gun control, more jobs, environmental protection, and for students, an effort to keep tuition costs in check [3] The President spoke about hiking the minimum wage to $9 an hour to help all low income families in these tough times, as well as investing $50 billion on rebuilding roads and bridges to create more jobs [4] Obama stated that growing the economy and creating good middle-class jobs "must be the North Star that guides our efforts," but he also laid out some other interesting proposals [5] He proposed that gun control should be talked about in light of recent tragedies [6] In addition, the President wants to beef up border protection and combine that with a law to create a path for undocumented immigrant students to become legal U [7] S [8] citizens [9] For education reform, he proposed rewards for effective schools, coupled with encouragement to follow their own curricula and methods [10] In addition, he stated that there should be State laws that require students to stay in school until graduation or age 18 [11] For those in college, he wants to extend the college tuition tax credit and double the number of work-study jobs over the next five years [12] President Obama also wants to help the middle class by reforming the tax code [13] This includes the so-called Buffett rule, a minimum 30% tax rate for individuals whose income exceeds $1 million and no tax increases for anyone earning less than $250,000 [14] On the environmental side, he wants to reduce greenhouse gasses and produce more clean energy [15] Among these proposals is support for domestic natural gas production, with regulations on chemicals used in the extraction process [16] This would also include the passage of clean energy tax credits and an end to subsidies for the fossil fuel industry [17] He wants to develop enough clean energy on public land to power three million homes and help manufacturers reduce energy waste in factories [18] Finally, one of the more important things the president wants to do is create a more bipartisan Government, and he challenged the current Congress to get up and get to work on the nation by having both parties work together [19]
